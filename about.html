    Joshiana 13.0 | 2024

    Joshiana 13.0 is a National level IT fest organized by the Department of Computer Applications in association with TECHNOCRATS, the student Association of MCA of St. Joseph Engineering College, Mangaluru. Explore The Unseen ,Innovate The Future. So, we welcome you to the theme of Joshiana 13.0 - SCIENCE FICTION which means - ‘Journey Beyond Reality to Unveil What Could Be and What Will Come.’. So come take a walk through our SCIENCE FICTION and experience the simulation on the 29th and 30th of August 2024 at St. Joseph Engineering College.
